Lions

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. Lions live in groups called prides and are native to Africa. They are powerful predators with a majestic roar.

Fox

Foxes are small, agile mammals known for their cunning nature and bushy tails. Red foxes are the most common species and are found across the Northern Hemisphere.

Tiger

Tigers are the largest members of the cat family. Recognized by their bold black stripes, they are powerful hunters native to Asia and are excellent swimmers.

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest land animals in the world. With their long necks and legs, they browse treetops for food and move gracefully across African savannahs.

Elephants

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence, strong memory, and emotional depth. They use their trunks for communication and feeding.

Penguins

Penguins are flightless birds that live mostly in the Southern Hemisphere. They're excellent swimmers and are known for their tuxedo-like appearance and waddling walk.
