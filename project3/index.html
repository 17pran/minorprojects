Book Review

The Diary of a Young Girl by Anne Frank

Published July 2, 2025

Tags: #Biography #WWII #AnneFrank

About the Book

"The Diary of a Young Girl" was written by Anne Frank while hiding during the Nazi occupation of the Netherlands. It was published in 1952 in English and became one of the most important books of the 20th century.

The copyright of the Dutch version expired in 2016, seventy years after Anne's death.

Introduction to the Diary

Anne Frank wrote her diary as letters to "Kitty", a fictional friend. After hearing a radio broadcast encouraging people to document their wartime experiences, Anne rewrote her entries to prepare for future publication.

She wrote about her personal feelings, relationships, and the fear of being discovered while in hiding. Her words show a deep desire to be heard and understood.

My Thoughts

This diary is powerful, emotional, and inspiring. Anne's words feel honest and brave. Her thoughts are still meaningful today and remind us of the importance of human rights and hope.
